Mapping Different Definitions of Sensitive Communities

To better understand the potential impacts of adopting different definitions of "sensitive communities" in SB 50, we map each definition and overlay those areas with the transit-rich areas defined by the bill. A fifth option, "Tracts In All Definitions" is included to show tracts that qualify as "sensitive communities" in all four potential definitions used in this research.

Each definition is included on the map as a separate layer than can be toggled on and off using the legend in the top-left corner of the map. Users can search for locations using the magnifying glass in the bottom-right corner of the map.
